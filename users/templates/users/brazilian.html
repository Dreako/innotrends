Brazilian Blowout

by: Love Fussion


Benefits of Brazilian Blowout:

  • Restore damage hair.
  • Hair will be shiny, silky, smooth and straight with volume.
  • Improve the texture and condition of the ends.

    • HOW TO APPLY:

  • Wash Hair with any profesional clarifying shampoo.
  • Blow dry up to 70-90% dry, Section hair then evenly apply Brazilian Restoration Cream onto section hair.(Notice: keep 1 cm away from hair ends), later comb hair evenly. Leave Cream 30-60 minutes onto the hair or until 100% dry.
  • Rinse hair lightly with warm water. (DO NOT Shampoo hair).
  • Blowdry hair until 100% dry.
  • Section hair then use iron straightener. VERY IMPORTANT: Select appropriate flat iron temperature below: 450 degrees fahrentheit (232 degrees Celcius) for normal hair. 430 degrees fahrentheit (221 degrees Celcius) for fine hair. 350-400 degrees fahrentheit (177-204 Celcius) for over-bleached hair.
  • Leave hair out of water for 2-3 days.
