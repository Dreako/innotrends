Starbeans Coffee

by: Innotrends


12 in 1 INGREDIENTS:

  • NON DAIRY CREAMER
  • COFFEE POWDER
  • MANGOSTEEN EXTRACT
  • AGARICUS MUSHROOM
  • GARCINIA CAMBOGIA
  • GREEN TEA EXTRACT
  • GLUTATHIONE
  • GINGKO BILOBA
  • COLLAGEN
  • STEVIA
  • CINNAMON
  • ACAI BERRY

DIRECTIONS

  • HOT - Empty contents of one sachet into cup add hot water. (approx.150 ml.) stir well and server.
  • Cold - Dissolve contents of water one sachet in a small amount of hot water. Fill cup glass with cold tap water (up to 150 ml.) stir well. add ice and serve.

    • STORAGE CONDITION

  • Store in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight, moisture and heat.

    • CAUTION

  • Not recommended for children, pregnant and lactating women.
